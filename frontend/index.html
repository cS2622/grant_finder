Welcome to our federal grant finder!

This site helps start-up founders, developers, and researchers find and apply for federal grants. To start exploring, please create a profile! After creating your profile, you can explore the grant finder page where you can explore grants based on your industry interests.

After creating your profile, you can also explore the connections page where you can find other users with similar industry interests. This is a great way to find other industry professionals based on interest, location, and more.
